Bubblekitty

Brand Identity/Redesign, Packaging, and Marketing Material

Bubblekitty is a boba brand identity project catering to an audience with a love for cute and sweet aesthetics. Packaging was assigned to the brief afterwards, but not knowing how to incorporate that specific type of packaging into a boba brand, I decided to go with merchandise that customers could prepare at home. The milk tea and dried boba mixes were based on cute snack packaging found in Asian markets, utlizing illustrations that I had made for the brand.
