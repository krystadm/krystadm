Bubblekitty

Brand Identity, Packaging, and Visual Design

Bubblekitty is a boba brand identity project. Packaging was assigned to the brief afterwards, but not knowing how to incorporate that specific type of packaging into a boba brand, I decided to go with products that customers could prepare at home.

I am currently expanding on this work with its brand redesign, and am working on implementing a mobile ordering experience. Please check back soon for more! :)
