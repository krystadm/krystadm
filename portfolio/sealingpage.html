Sealing Technologies

Marketing Design, Brand Identity, UI/UX, Research

As one of SealingTech's first in-house designers and marketing coordinators, it was my goal to produce a flexible and consistent visual image for the company as we developed a marketing strategy. In December of 2019, SealingTech acquired Quark, which gave the opportunity to modernize the company's look. I developed a brand guide based on mutual themes, and incorporated new ideas to help transition SealingTech into modernizing their look over time.

Website Redesign Overview

SealingTech's top priority was to modernize and establish a proper web presence. Along with the acquisition of Quark and their Linux and Mobile-based security capabilities, we needed a strategy to enter the commercial hardware/software industry. The previously-made informational company website needed to be completely revamped as a proper brand identity was not established, the organization of information was a challenge to navigate, and new content needed to be developed.

Goals:
  • Create Brand Assimilation
  • Highlight Thought Leadership and Service Lifecycle
  • Increase Site Traffic and optimize content for SEO
  • Establish E-Commerce Platform for Products
  • Section 508 Accessibility
