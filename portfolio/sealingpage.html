Sealing Technologies

Marketing Design & Strategy, Brand Identity, UI/UX

As one of SealingTech's first in-house hybrid designer/marketing coordinators, it was my goal to produce a flexible and consistent visual image for the company as we developed strategies to bring in more leads within the B2G industry. My responsibility was developing a new design system in effort of revamping past and future collateral, as well as designing for web content, landing pages, and email campaigns. The SealingTech marketing team has been successful in launching a website redesign, product campaigns, and increasing annual profits.

Website Redesign Overview

SealingTech's top priority was to modernize and establish a proper web presence. We needed to fix the issues applying to the old website which was roughly built on Wordpress CMS, and address customer and employee concerns regarding the organization and design of the website.

Issues:
  • No trace of brand identity (logos, fonts, colors)
  • Confusing navigation
  • Unoptimized content
  • Unclear journey for customers/applicants
  • No CRM Connection
  • Lack of contrast and hierarchy

    • Research

    We conducted research on large-scale defense contractors and hardware companies with similar B2G and B2B services, like Raytheon and Dell, to get an idea of effective digital marketing and customer journey/retention strategies in the Federal industry. We found that the most effective way to track these journeys and generate leads was through a C2A strategy for product quotes and spec sheet downloads followed by a drip campaign strategy through Salesforce and Pardot connectors.
