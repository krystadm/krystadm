Sealing Technologies, Inc.

Marketing Design & Strategy, Brand Identity, UI/UX

SealingTech is a service provider and contractor based in the DC-Metro area that offers cybersecurity-based engineering services, as well as and hardware and software products within the federal defense sector. I was lucky to be able join their newly-established marketing team to jumpstart my design career, and since our team was small, there was overlap which worked in my benefit to help me find my core strengths as a designer.

In addition to providing design for the marketing team, I worked with stakeholders, engineers, and industry partners by providing technical graphics for proposals, content copywriting for web and social media, and performed user and market research.

Brand and Marketing Design

It was my goal to produce a flexible and consistent visual image for the company as we developed strategies to bring in more leads within the B2G industry. My responsibility was developing a new design system in effort of revamping past and future collateral, as well as designing for web content, landing pages, and email campaigns.

The SealingTech marketing team has been successful in launching a website redesign, implementing marketing automation, and product campaigns, resulting in an increase in leads, content and social media engagement, employment, and providing a better sales and user journey.

Website Redesign

SealingTech's top priority was to modernize and establish a proper web presence to generate leads, automate sales processes, increase engagement, and provide a straightforward journey for applicants. We needed to fix the issues applying to the old website which was roughly built on Wordpress CMS, and address customer and employee concerns regarding the organization and design of the website, and rather than rearrange content, we decided to overhaul the entire website and experience.
