Warm&Cozy

Brand Identity, Packaging, and Marketing Material

Warm&Cozy is a 4-piece seasonal spice gift set which invokes the comfy essences of Wintertime through the warm notes of vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, and allspice. Each label is a warm welcome to each spice's native plant through subtle color and detail, which come together through a cozy bouquet of botanical illustrations.
