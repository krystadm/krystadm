MOODYKIT

Branding, Packaging Design, Illustration, and Print

MoodyKit was a 2019 Senior Capstone Project involving brand identity, product and packaging design. I created 4 boxes based on each season in subscription format, which included a centralized design. Each kit contained stationery, stickers, postcards, candy, and a facial mask. Each seasonal theme would not focus on a conventional stereotype, but the "edgy" and unconventional parts of each season.
