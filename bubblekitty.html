Bubblekitty

Branding, Packaging, Illustration, and Print

Bubblekitty is a brand identity project focused on a fictional boba shop I made up. I was tasked with creating a logo and following a styleguide, and eventually made 2 cube box products. I gave a focus on bright pastel colors and a simple vector illustrations. The use of serif typefaces balances out the childish themes of cute illustrations and patterns in order to fit a flexible target audience.
