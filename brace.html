Brace

Tri-Fold Brochure Design, Photography, and Print

The goal of this project was to create a trifold informational brochure while retaining the brand identity created as a group. The biggest challenge in this was creating a target audience less relatable to myself as a person, so I picked a "handyman" key demographic and studied the look of modern home improvement graphics to understand the key aspects which would appeal to my target audience. Bold typography and and rustic imagery were used as key details while I sampled the colors and textures from a construction workshop to use as a background for the informational parts
