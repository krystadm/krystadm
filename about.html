About Me

Krysta Madewell

I'm a Fil-Am designer who was born and raised in the SF Bay Area, moved to every corner of California, and always ended up back in the Bay like a magnet. Digital art and old-school web design were some of my favorite creative hobbies growing up. After exploring different roadmaps like game design, animation, and marketing design under the Media Arts program at Santa Barbara City College, I set off back home (again) to CSU East Bay, where I received a BA in Graphic Design.

I started my career in a hybrid role as a design team (of one) and marketing associate in the Defense Contracting industry. By the end, I transitioned to more technical teams and projects, and that's when I found an excitement that I had never felt upon learning UI and IX.

In 2021, the magnetic forces of the Bay Area called upon me again to come home and challenge myself to use my skills to design for impact.
