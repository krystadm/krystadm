KRYSTA MADEWELL.

Visual Design | Brand Identity | Web | UX/UI | Illustration

I'm a multifaceted designer from the Bay Area, California with passions for food, video games, technology, and my Shiba Inu, Shinjiro. I flocked towards the east coast in 2019 to explore the cyber industry, and am currently the creative head at Sealing Technologies, Inc.

My focuses include visual, brand identity, and UI/UX design, accompanied by an understanding of best practices and customer studies based on my experiences in digital marketing. My skills and experiences have fallen under multiple hats between both design and marketing, giving me the advantage of knowing the full scope of effective visual marketing as well as user-centric thinking.

I thrive off of learning about others and designing for experiences that promote simplicity, community, and joy.

And yes, I keep it Madewell.
