About Me

Krysta M.

Just a FilAm, born raised in the SF Bay, who grew into a self-explanitory designer. My childhood hobbies consisted of gaming, tinkering with dad's spare work laptops, and learning how to customize things like forum signatures and webpages. Along that line, I found a passion for multimedia arts, venturing into every medium I could. There was no doubt that I'd be a designer today.

I started my career and education in digital design, jumping around in different concentrations, but I've always been drawn to interactivity.

Today, I am endlessly working with teams to create meaningful experiences, establishing systems, and sometimes being a boundary-pushing creative if the opportunity alllows. :-)
