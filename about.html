KRYSTA D. MADEWELL


I'm a visual designer from the Bay Area, California, fully equipped with a sense of humor and a passion for food, video games, technology, and my Shiba Inu, Shinjiro. I graduated in 2019 from CSU East Bay, with a BA in Graphic Design after transferring in 2017 with a Multimedia AA from Santa Barbara City College where I also studied Digital Marketing and Web Design. I flocked towards the east coast after graduating in 2019 to explore the Federal IT industry, and am currently the creative head at Sealing Technologies, Inc. for all things digital design and social media marketing.

My focuses include web, branding, as well as both digital and traditional marketing design, making it my goal to establish an image that is consistent across all channels, communicates effectively, and represents the cause. My skills and experiences have fallen under multiple hats, giving me the advantage of understanding the ins-and-outs of the "5 W's", and what my role as a designer plays in such circumstances.

Since design will always be consistently changing, I come with the motivation to always adapt to it, emphasizing the importance of learning from the world and endlessly improving my knowledge as a designer who strives to live up to a "Madewell" standard.
