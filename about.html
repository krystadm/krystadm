Krysta Madewell

Designer | Amateur Chef | Lowkey Gamer

I am a hapa/halfie/mixed Fil-Am designer who was born and raised in the SF Bay Area, moved to every corner of California, and always ended up back in the Bay like a magnet. Digital art and old-school web design were some of my favorite creative hobbies growing up. After exploring different roadmaps like game design, animation, and marketing design under the Media Arts program at Santa Barbara City College, I set off back home (again) to CSU East Bay, where I received a BA in Graphic Design.

Somewhere along the post-grad line, I moved to the DC Metro area, providing visual, RFP, and content marketing solutions within the DoD as well as freelance design/art services to keep the variety going. Through my hybrid role, I found a passion for research, information architecture, and accessiblity, then became obsessed with the idea of UX and design.

As the magnetic forces of home call upon me again, I am currently open to new UX/Visual Design opportunities in the Bay Area.
