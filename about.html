About Me

Krysta Madewell

I'm a Filipino-American–born & raised in the SF Bay. Childhood hobbies of drawing anime and tinkering with my dad's spare laptops transitioned into learning HTML and design software as a form of self-expression in the eras of Neopets and MySpace. This was the destined path that led me to earning a BA in Design at CSUEB and becoming a designer.

I started my career doing hybrid design and marketing. I found a knack for working with engineering and product teams, and that's when I found a passion for designing experiences.

When I'm not designing, I am playing FFXIV, spending time with my dog, and cooking.
