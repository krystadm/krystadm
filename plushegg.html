PLUSHEGG: S'18 Pinback Cards

Illustration, Character Design, Pinback Card

I had a chance to work with Plushegg, a San Diego-based pin company, to help conceptualize and incorporate the Plushegg mascot into their Summer 2018 series. I was fully allowed to redesign the character in my own art style, changing the proportions and features while still retaining the original concept and theme of the character. This illustration would then be vectorized and divided into different pin backings, which would then reveal the full image when aligned.
