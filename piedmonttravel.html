PIEDMONT

Booklet Design, Photography and Print

Pacific Travel was a brand identity created in collaboration with my Production Design class in which we picked parks from an individual city and made a travel booklet. I was tasked with documenting aesthetically pleasing parks in the small town of Piedmont, CA, which shares the northern border of Oakland, CA. I focused on key visuals and family-friendly activities.
