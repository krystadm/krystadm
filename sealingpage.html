ST Product Page

Website Redesign, Brand Identity, Product Mockup

A cybersecurity business had wanted me to demonstrate a more "digestible" way of describing their services and what their products were in mockup form. They were not utilizing branding guidelines, so I created a consistent branding guide based on their social media logo which I would stick to for the rest of development. Since the original page was quickly thrown together using a site builder, I had redesigned and organized all of the content based on current design trends for tech businesses. I changed the informational hierarchy to something that would make more sense on a marketing aspect: why they do it, what they offer, and reliable information. Along with redoing all visual content from scratch, I also had to demonstrate copywriting skills to accompany the informational hierarchy and correct some of the errors in their previous sections.
