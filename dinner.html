DINNER

Book Sleeve Design, Illustration, and Print

I was tasked to create a publishing identity with a group and create various book jackets based on an assigned selection of podcasts. It was my first experimentation with a book sleeve and there were challenging aspects to how I could introduce a story through visual detail without giving too much away. Since it focused on two orphaned brothers, I wanted to focus on how they bonded but leave the transparency open so it could be relatable to anyone taking interest in it, in relation to the "stickmen" type figures and neutral colors. A handwriting script font would detail a familiar feeling of handwritten notes.
