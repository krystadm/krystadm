Making it Madewell.

Hello, I’m Krysta Madewell! I’m a designer who iterates with the end-goal of creating an allyship through user-centered design. I have 2 years experience as a hybrid creative with a strong background in visual design and product marketing for high-growth federal and enterprise solution providers in the IT industry.

  • Former Designer @ SealingTech. Currently looking for full-time UX/UI roles!

    • I build well-made solutions through...

    Cross-functional Design

    I have flexible roots working with engineering, marketing, and product-oriented teams in a diverse, collective effort to grow and meet user satisfaction.

    An Empathic Approach

    My priority as a designer is to create solutions by fully understanding my customer's motivations, problems, and insights to create optimal solutions.

    Equity & Engagement

    I acknowledge that the principles of design play a huge part in how users resonate and engage with a product. I value designing inclusive and informative journeys for all.

    Continuous Improvement

    I'm equipped with the mindset that delivery is never "final". I aim to grow alongside my work and understand the insights and data to always improve upon solutions.
